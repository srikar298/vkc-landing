# Phase 4: Strategic Future (PM Perspective)

**Target Timeline**: Year 2+

## Objective
World-class digital ecosystem with direct financial impact via E-Commerce and Crowdfunding.

## Strategic Value
* **Complete Lifecycle Support**: Move from "listing" artisans to actively selling their goods and funding workshops.
* **Monetization**: Transaction fees from D2C e-commerce and angel networks ensure VKC sustainability.

## Feature Evaluation & Edge Cases
* **E-Commerce Logistics**: Start with local-pickup only, then integrate shipping aggregators.
* **Scheme Auto-Apply**: Killer feature leveraging saved auto-generating PDF applications.
