# Phase 3: Content & Community Support (PM Perspective)

**Target Timeline**: Months 5-6

## Objective
Drive daily active usage and retention via content and essential services.

## Strategic Value
* **Retention Engine**: News and educational updates drive daily app opens.
* **Data Acquisition**: Track popular scholarships/jobs to tailor offline initiatives.

## Feature Evaluation & Edge Cases
* **Content Operations**: Aggregate RSS feeds or ensure dedicated volunteer CMS updates to prevent stale content.
* **Matrimony Risks**: Keep strictly MVP or delay entirely due to severe trust and privacy compliance requirements.
